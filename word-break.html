文字换行与截断

默认【正常】

默认正常文字和数字2021年，中国最好的一年；2022年，也是。

默认【错误】

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

word-wrap: break-word;

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

如果单词太长会自动换行。

overflow-wrap: break-word;

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

功能上同，W3C 新写法。

overflow-wrap: anywhere;

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

任何地方都可以换行

white-space: nowrap;

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

段落中的文本不换行（会截断）。

word-break: keep-all;

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

【默认】单词放不下会自动换行【文字截断】。

word-break: break-all;

12345678901234567890any anywhere anywhereanywhereanywhere

单词放不下会折断换行，铺满元素【文字截断】。
